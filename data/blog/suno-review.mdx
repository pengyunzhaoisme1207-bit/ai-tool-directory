## Overview

Suno AI is an AI music generation platform that creates complete songs — vocals, instruments, and lyrics — from a simple text description. Its v4 model (2025) delivers remarkably coherent song structures with verse, chorus, and bridge sections that rival amateur studio recordings.

## Key Features

- **Full Song Generation**: Describe a mood, genre, or scene, and Suno generates a complete 2-4 minute song with vocals and instrumentation.
- **Custom Lyrics**: Write your own lyrics or let the AI generate them. Specify language, theme, and tone.
- **Genre Presets**: Choose from 40+ genres including pop, rock, country, EDM, hip-hop, classical, jazz, and many more.
- **Instrumental Mode**: Generate backing tracks without vocals — useful for podcasts, videos, and background music.
- **Extend**: Take an existing song and add new verses, a bridge, or an outro.
- **Commercial Rights**: Paid plans include commercial licensing for the songs you generate.

## Pros and Cons

**Pros:**
- Song quality is shockingly good — coherent structure, natural vocals
- Huge genre variety with consistent quality across styles
- Daily free credits make it easy to experiment
- Commercial rights on paid plans open up real business use cases
- Extend feature lets you iteratively build longer compositions

**Cons:**
- Free tier does not allow commercial use
- Lyrics can sometimes be cliched or repetitive
- Limited control over specific musical elements (key, tempo, chord progression)
- Vocal clarity varies by genre — works best for pop and rock
- Copyright questions around AI-generated music are still unresolved legally

## Verdict

Suno AI is the most impressive AI music tool available. Whether you are a musician looking for inspiration, a content creator needing background music, or just someone who wants to hear a song about their cat — Suno delivers. The Pro plan at $10/month with commercial rights is excellent value.
