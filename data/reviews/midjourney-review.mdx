## Overview

Midjourney remains the quality leader in AI image generation. V7, released in April 2025 and now the default model, delivers richer textures, significantly better coherence for hands and bodies, and two new game-changing features: Draft Mode for rapid iteration and Personalization for taste-aligned outputs.

## Key Features

- **V7 Model**: Substantial quality leap with beautiful textures and coherent detail in bodies, hands, and objects
- **Draft Mode**: 10x faster rendering at half the cost — perfect for exploring ideas quickly
- **Personalization**: AI learns your aesthetic preferences after ranking ~200 image pairs (~5 minutes setup)
- **Omni Reference**: Upload any image as a reference — V7 interprets composition, style, and mood simultaneously
- **Style Reference Codes (SREF)**: Replicate and share exact style configurations across your team
- **Voice Mode**: Speak your ideas in Draft Mode and watch images flow in real-time

## API Performance

- **API Access**: Limited beta API launched in 2025, expanding access
- **Generation Speed**: Draft Mode ~5-10 seconds; Standard ~30-60 seconds; Turbo ~15-20 seconds
- **Output Quality**: 1024x1024 default; up to 2048x2048 with upscale
- **Rate Limits**: Based on GPU hours per plan; relax mode unlimited on Standard+

## Privacy & Safety

- **Stealth Mode**: Pro+ plans generate private images not visible in the community feed
- **Commercial License**: All paid plans include full commercial usage rights
- **Content Policy**: Strict NSFW filter; brand-safe by default
- **Data Retention**: Generated images stored on Midjourney servers; no deletion API yet

## The Killer Feature

**V7 + Draft Mode + Personalization** — the combination is unbeatable for creative exploration. Draft Mode lets you iterate 10x faster, exploring dozens of concepts in minutes. Personalization ensures results align with your taste. When you find the one, hit "enhance" to render it at full V7 quality. For designers, this is the fastest path from vague idea to polished visual asset.

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Best-in-class artistic quality and texture
- Draft Mode enables rapid creative exploration
- Personalization learns your aesthetic over time
- Strong community and prompt-sharing culture

**Cons:**
- No free tier (starting at $10/month)
- Discord-based workflow feels dated compared to web apps
- Closed-source — no self-hosting or API customization
- Edit and upscale still partially fall back to V6 models

## Verdict

Midjourney V7 is the reigning champion of AI image quality. If you need the best possible visuals for presentations, marketing, or creative projects, nothing else matches V7''s texture and coherence. The $10/month entry price is justified by Draft Mode alone — the speed improvement makes it genuinely practical for iterative design work.
