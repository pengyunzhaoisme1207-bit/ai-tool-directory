## Overview

Perplexity AI has evolved from an AI-powered search engine into a full answer platform. In 2026, it launched Perplexity Computer — an autonomous agent that orchestrates 19+ AI models, executes complex workflows, and integrates directly into enterprise tools like Slack, Salesforce, and Snowflake. With its own proprietary search index (no longer reliant on third-party APIs), Perplexity is the most capable research tool available.

## Key Features

- **Perplexity Computer**: Autonomous AI agent that plans and executes multi-step research workflows
- **Deep Research**: State-of-the-art on Deep Search QA and Scale AI Research Rubric, powered by Opus 4.5 for Max users
- **Model Council**: Run three frontier models simultaneously, compare outputs, get higher-confidence answers
- **Proprietary Search Index**: No longer dependent on external APIs — AI-optimized search built in-house
- **Enterprise**: Slack/Teams integration, Salesforce/Snowflake/SharePoint connectors, SOC 2 Type II
- **Comet Browser Agent**: Powered by Opus 4.6, handles complex web tasks like booking, form-filling, research

## Architecture & Model Specs

- **Multi-Model Orchestration**: Routes queries to optimal model from 19+ available (Claude, GPT, Gemini, etc.)
- **Search Index**: Proprietary AI-optimized index, replacing third-party search APIs
- **Memory Engine**: Creates richer memories, recalls details from past conversations across sessions
- **Context**: Full conversation history with document and file attachments

## API Performance

- **API Access**: Available for developers, third-party integrations
- **Response Time**: 2-4s for standard search; 30-120s for Deep Research
- **Rate Limits**: Pro: generous daily limits; Max: unlimited Deep Research
- **Subagents**: GPT-5.3-Codex for coding tasks, specialized models for finance, health, legal

## The Killer Feature

**Perplexity Computer + Deep Research** — describe a complex research task ("analyze the competitive landscape of AI coding tools and produce a comparison report"), and Computer orchestrates search across multiple sources, reads and synthesizes dozens of articles, cross-references data, and produces a cited document. For Max users, Deep Research with Opus 4.5 achieves state-of-the-art performance on external benchmarks. Combined with Model Council (three models running in parallel), you get the most reliable, hallucination-free answers available.

## Best Use Cases

Perplexity is strongest when the answer needs current sources. It is useful for market scans, product comparisons, policy changes, competitor research, vendor discovery and executive briefings where a traditional chatbot may answer fluently but without enough evidence.

It also fits teams that need a research layer between search and writing. A user can start with a broad question, inspect citations, ask follow-up questions and turn the result into a brief. That workflow is faster than manually opening ten tabs, but still more verifiable than asking a general assistant to rely on memory.

## Where Perplexity Needs Verification

Perplexity's citations make it safer than many chatbots, but they do not remove the need for review. Users should still open sources for pricing, legal claims, product terms and anything that affects a purchasing decision. A cited source can be outdated, promotional or only partially relevant.

For enterprise use, the key question is whether the team needs source-backed answers or agentic web actions. The Pro plan is usually enough for individual research. Max and Computer-style workflows make more sense when the user repeatedly runs deep research, competitive analysis or multi-step web tasks.

## Perplexity vs ChatGPT

Use Perplexity when freshness and citations are the priority. Use ChatGPT when the task is broader: drafting, reasoning, coding help, spreadsheet analysis or multi-format content work. Many teams benefit from both because they solve different problems.

A practical workflow is to research in Perplexity, then move verified notes into a writing or planning assistant. This keeps the factual layer separate from the synthesis layer and reduces the risk of publishing unsupported claims.

## Verdict

Perplexity AI is the definitive AI research tool in 2026. The multi-model orchestration, Deep Research with Opus 4.5, and proprietary search index make it the most reliable source for fact-checked, cited answers. The $20/mo Pro plan is excellent value; Max is for power users who need agentic workflows.
