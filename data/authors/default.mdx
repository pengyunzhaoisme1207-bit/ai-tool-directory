## What We Do

AI Tool Directory is an independent review platform dedicated to helping you find the right AI tools for your needs. We publish in-depth reviews, head-to-head comparisons, and roundups of the latest AI products across categories including coding assistants, search engines, image generators, writing tools, and productivity apps.

## Our Mission

The AI tool landscape changes rapidly — new products launch every week, and existing ones evolve constantly. Our goal is to cut through the noise and provide honest, detailed, hands-on evaluations so you can make informed decisions without spending hours testing every tool yourself.

## How We Review

Every review on this site follows a consistent process:

1. **Hands-On Testing** — We actually use each tool for real-world tasks, not just browse the marketing page. We test the free tier, paid features, and edge cases.
2. **Feature Analysis** — We break down what the tool does well, where it falls short, and who it is best suited for.
3. **Pricing Evaluation** — We document current pricing plans and assess whether the tool offers good value for money.
4. **Comparisons** — When relevant, we compare similar tools side by side so you can see the trade-offs clearly.

## Our Review Criteria

| Criteria | What We Look For |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Ease of Use** | Can a beginner get value quickly? Is the interface intuitive? |
| **Output Quality** | How good are the results? Are they reliable and consistent? |
| **Features** | Does it go beyond basics? Any unique capabilities? |
| **Pricing** | Is the free tier useful? Are paid plans fairly priced? |
| **Performance** | How fast does it respond? Any reliability issues? |
| **Privacy** | How does it handle your data? Any privacy concerns? |
